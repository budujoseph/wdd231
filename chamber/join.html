Membership Levels

Non Profit Membership Level

Non Profit Membership

Benefits include:

  • Build relationships with local business, potential donors and volunteers.
  • Access training on nonprofit management, marketing, leadership and fundraising.
Cost: $0

Bronze Membership Level

Bronze Membership Level

Benefits include:

  • Get featured on the chamber's website and printed materials.
  • Stay informed and involved in legislative issues that impact businesses.
  • Collaborate with local businesses for joint fundraising events, volunteer programs and cause driven initiatives.
Cost: $50.00

Silver Membership Level

Silver Membership Level

Benefits include:

  • Increase frequency of mentions on the chamber's social media platforms, reaching a wider audience.
  • Access to more exclusive or high-level networking events, including mixers luncheons, and business expos.
  • Offers voting rights for members, giving you a say in the direction and decisions of the chamber.
  • Get exclusive data, surveys and reports that can help you stay informed about local economic conditions, potential donors and community trends.
Cost: $80.00

Gold Membership Level

Gold Membership Level

Benefits include:

  • Receives the most prominent placement in the chamber's online and printed directories, ensuring maximum exposure.
  • Access to high level invite-only events such as exclusive luncheons with top business leaders, government officials and philanthropic investors
  • Invitations to private meetings and discussions with key business figures, local policy makers and influencers.
  • Gain access to more comprehensive group insurance options, including health, liability and worker's compensation coverage for employees.
  • Recognition in front of the local business and nonprofit community, as well as press releases and chamber communications
Cost: $120.00
